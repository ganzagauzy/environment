About

Us

Rweru Environmental Concern is a community based organisation to protect environmental and water bodies while improving the life style of the people around lake Rweru

Our work promotes policies to improve the economical and social well-being of people around the lake rweru. The Rweru Environmental Concern (RECO) brings together community leaders, civil organisations and academics to seek solutions to common problems of environmental protection, develop global standards, share experiences.


Mr.Habimana Diogene will be known as the founder of Rweru Environmental Concern(RECO) and team leader of the RECO, the organisation serving as its primary spokesperson.

Mr.Habimana Diogene will be a full-time public advocate for the Rweru Environmental Concern(RECO).



Mr.Habimana Diogene has a Bachelor's degreein Tourism Management from Kabale University (Uganda).

Our

Vision

Our vision recognizes the environmental issues facing society such as climate change, water bodies protection, environental pollution, and destruction of the ecosystem as business risks for the communities and commitment to solving these issues over the long term.

Our

Mission

Rweru Environmental Concern have a mission to protect, restore and enhance environmental quality towards good public health, environmental integrity and economic viability.

More

to know

Rweru Environmental Concern will work on the main environmental challenges facing Rwanda, which are :

  • Land scarcity
  • Soil degradation and soil erosion
  • Deforestation
  • Climate change
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Water pollution and access
  • Urban pollution and natural resourcespressures
  • Generation of hazardous and solid waste
