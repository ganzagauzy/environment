About

Us

Rweru Environmental Concern is a community based organisation to protect environmental and water bodies while improving the life style of the people around lac Rweru

Our work promotes policies to improve the economical and social well-being of people around the lac rweru. The Rweru Environmental Concern (RECO) brings together community leaders, civil organisationsand academics to seek solutions to common problems of environmental protection, develop global standards, share experiences.

to know

Rweru Environmental Concern will work on the main environmental challenges facing Rwanda, which are :

  • Land scarcity
  • Soil degradation and soil erosion
  • Deforestation
  • Climate change
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Water pollution and access
  • Urban pollution and natural resourcespressures
  • Generation of hazardous and solid waste
